## Verdict
The CLI v2 produces clean nested boxes but lacks arrows, flow indicators, or semantic detail. Freehand adds flow arrows and hierarchy but is visually plain. The skill-enhanced version adds: bold pod borders (┏━┛), status indicators (● ◉ ◎), resource annotations (memory limits, replica counts), a PVC layer showing storage, shadow lines beneath deployments (░), and a legend box. The structural nature of this prompt limits how much artistic technique helps, but the skill version is clearly more informative and professional.
