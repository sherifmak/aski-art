## Verdict

**CLI:** Produces only the band name in a shadow font. No visual art capability whatsoever -- no sunset, no grid, no silhouettes.
**Freehand:** Delivers a complete album cover with gradient sunset, perspective grid road, palm tree silhouettes, retro-styled title, album name, track listing, and scan-line borders.
**Winner:** Freehand -- overwhelmingly. Album art is pure visual composition, which is entirely outside the CLI's scope.
