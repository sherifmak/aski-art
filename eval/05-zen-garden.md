## Verdict

**CLI:** Can only produce a text banner. The CLI has no scene-drawing or artistic composition capability whatsoever. The word "ZEN" in block letters is the absolute limit of what it can do for this prompt.
**Freehand:** A full scenic composition with raked sand patterns (using ~ and curvy characters), large and small rocks, a koi pond with water ripples, bamboo stalks with leaves, a stone lantern, stepping stones, a wooden bridge, moss patches, and a decorative wooden fence border. It captures the peaceful, meditative quality of a real karesansui garden.
**Winner:** Freehand -- this is the clearest example of where freehand ASCII art is irreplaceable. Artistic, scenic, and atmospheric content is completely outside the CLI's capabilities.
